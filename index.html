Why Cindi

I didn't come to this work because GLP-1s became popular. I came to it through my own body.

For nearly 30 years, I've lived with metabolic issues that forced me to pay attention. I had gestational diabetes. I've lived with Hashimoto's. I watched my A1C rise into the prediabetic range — despite knowing nutrition and doing everything I thought should work.
